Shaum

Project Link

The month of Ramadan for students is synonymous with the Ramadan journal which contains any activities carried out during the month of Ramadan. Usually the journal is in the form of a thin book which is distributed by the school and collected when Ramadhan is over. And usually students are always lazy to fill in the journal, not to mention if the journal is damaged, dirty, torn or even lost

Mirai Developer created an application called Shaum where all our activities will be carried out via Android or PC. We only need to log in with the account that has been determined and fill in the journal periodically. The stored data is more accurate and will not be lost. We also save on expenses for making books and only need to use the internet to access them.
